üîë Permissions Required

The app requires the following iOS permissions to function:

Local Network Access (Required)

Purpose: To discover and communicate with your UCR device on your local WiFi network

When Requested: When you first launch the app

What It Does:

  • Allows the app to scan for devices on your local network
  • Enables connection to your UCR device at its IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.1)
  • Permits Modbus TCP communication on port 502

What It Does NOT Do:

  • Does not access the internet
  • Does not connect to external servers
  • Does not share network information with third parties

How to Manage: You can control this permission at any time:
Go to: Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Local Network ‚Üí Remote Sensing System
Toggle the permission on or off

Note: Without this permission, the app cannot connect to your UCR device and will not function.

üîê Security & Data Protection

How We Protect Your Privacy

Since the app does not collect personal information, your privacy is protected by design:

  • Local-Only Communication: All data stays on your local network
  • No External Servers: The app never connects to external servers
  • No Data Collection: There is no user data to protect because none is collected
  • No User Accounts: No passwords or authentication credentials are stored
  • Direct Communication: Your device talks directly to your UCR controller

Your Responsibility

While the app itself doesn't collect data, we recommend:

  • Secure Your WiFi Network: Use WPA2 or WPA3 encryption with a strong password
  • Protect Your UCR Device: Follow Unitronics security best practices
  • Use on Trusted Networks: Only connect to the app on secure, trusted networks
  • Follow Company Policies: Adhere to your organization's industrial network security policies
  • Regular Updates: Keep your iOS device and the app updated for security patches
